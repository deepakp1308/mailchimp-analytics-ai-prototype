Common questions

How does Analytics AI access my data? +
Analytics AI reads only the data you connect — your Mailchimp account plus any integrations you authorize. It uses read-only access by default and shows you exactly which records it queries for each answer.
Will my data be used to train someone else's model? +
No. Your data is never used to train Analytics AI or any third-party model. It's used only to answer your questions in your account. Full details in our AI security brief.
What happens if Analytics AI is wrong? +
Every answer cites the rows and metrics it used — hover any number to verify. We also publish accuracy benchmarks quarterly. If you find an error, flag it and our team will investigate.
How long is the free trial? +
14 days, full access to Analytics AI features on the Standard plan. No credit card required to start. Cancel or downgrade any time.
Can I use Analytics AI with non-Mailchimp data? +
Yes. Connect Shopify, Stripe, Salesforce, your warehouse, or upload a CSV. Analytics AI works across whatever data you bring in.
What's the 70% intro discount? +
For new Standard plan customers, Analytics AI is 70% off for your first 3 months. After that, standard add-on pricing applies. Cancel anytime.
